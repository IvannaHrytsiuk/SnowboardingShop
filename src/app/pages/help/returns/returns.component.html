Return Policy

You may return your unused product for a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date.

You can expect a full refund to your credit card 7 to 14 days of our receiving your return. It may take up to 30 days to be visible on your credit card statement.

You can print a prepaid UPS shipping label or use another method of your choosing. Please note that if the UPS shipping label is used, $6.50 will be deducted from your return credit.
