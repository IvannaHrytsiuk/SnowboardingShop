When will my order ship?

All Orders typically ship within 48 hours once the order has been placed. Orders ship Monday through Friday excluding Holidays. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking number once the order has shipped from the warehouse.

Do you ship to my country?

We currently ship online orders only to locations inside the Europe. If you are interested in purchasing product outside of the Europe, please visit one of our following online stores or visit your local dealer. We cannot ship orders to a freight forwarding company.

For customers in South America, Australia or Asia please use the region chooser in the site navigation to find your country.

Payment & Billing:

Accepted Forms of Payment:

We accept all major credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, Burton Flagship Store Gift Cards, PayPal, AmazonPay and Klarna Pay It in 4. No coins please. We are also unable to process Visa or MasterCard Prepaid Gift Cards

Timing of Billing:

Your credit card will only be charged once your order has shipped. If your order ships from different warehouses, you may see multiple charges on your credit card. These multiple charges will total the original value of your order.

Out of Stock Items:

If an item ordered becomes out of stock, you will receive an email detailing the item in question. You will not be charged for the item.
